About Me

I’m pretty sure you don’t want to read much. So, I will keep it as simple as possible and in case you would like to know more about me you can go here.

I’ve always had the desire for creating. If I’m sure about one thing about myself is that I love it and is very difficult for me to not do it. I can remember myself designing shoes and perfume bottles at the age of 4. One design was a perfume with the form of a light bulb, to my impression many years after I saw a brand selling a perfume with the exact same design.

I would have loved to turns this ideas into reality at the time. Maybe that’s why I got hooked the first time I saw an awesome website created by a friend in school at the age of 11. Since then I got fascinated by the idea of creating one. At the age of 14 with not much social life I started created my first HTML file in a text document.

And so, my journey as a developer started. Who would’ve thought that 3 years laters I would had a MMORPG server in Chile a modified version of the Tibia CipSoft game. My community was so popular at the time that it was shown in national television, obviously it collapsed and crashed, the amount of users in the forum has surpass the thousand online.

I’m not sure if programming itself is my passion, I would say is a combination of things. Coding is a catalyst for creation. To be able to portrai a complex idea that was taking from the intangible space of imagination into reality is fascinating.

This feeling is something I always try to tap into in all my projects. If I have to be honest I am not persuing money. I’m more interested in enjoying the process and challenge of the creation itself. This is where I want to be. In the world of ideas, logic and mental laberinth.
